About Me

I am an Egyptian-American Electrical and Computer Engineering student with a huge passion for everything computer related. Ever since I was a kid in Egypt using the family computer as his only point of contact with his father in America, computers have had a special place in my heart.

1 / 3
I spent most of my high school career and nearly every cent I made from work saving to purchase parts to build my own PC.
2 / 3
My interest in computers continued to expand past software and eventually led me to take two classes on computer architecture. For my final project in one of these classes, I got to design and simulate a 8-bit CPU down to the wires and logic gates in SystemVerilog.
3 / 3
I continued to become interested in more low level computing and took a class in circuits where I was able to look at how computers work on a microscopic level and understand the math behind electricity.
